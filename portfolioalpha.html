Ying Wang

I am an incoming PhD student at NYU Center for Data Science advised by Prof. Andrew Wilson and Prof. Mengye Ren. I received my MSDS at NYU as well. Prior to moving to New York, I studied Computer Science, Statistics, and Finance at McGill University in Montreal, Canada.

My primary research interest is multimodal learning. Specifically, I aim to build systems that can emulate human-like perception by seamlessly integrating various modalities such as text, images, videos and audio.
